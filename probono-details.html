Pro Bono

Pro Bono is a company that aimed to speak to one of the U.N.'s 17 sustainable development goals, particularly around peace, justice, and strong institutions. Created in a day as part of a Hackathon, our team created a site that would allow users to receive legal assistance through a forum where lawyers could post advice.
